ABOUT

Brief History Of Idoma Children Outreach Project

Idoma Christian Association (ICA) Kaduna Branch was the one that gave birth to the present Idoma Children Outreach project nineteen years ago (2005) we had six of the children camp project successfully in Trinity Methodist Church until 2007 when we had to stop due to insecurity in the land but resumed in 2016.

Aims and Objectives

The project derived it's aim from Romans 10:1 "Brethren our heart desire and prayer to God is for Isreal(Idoma Children), to know God and be saved." In view of this fact, we are labouring and mobilizing the Idoma Christians to reach out to Idoma children, with the gospel, and to Train them on how to read the Idoma Language Bible that we now have in our generation.

Activities

Our activities to fulfill our mandate include: Children Camp, Rallies, Seminars, Christmas Special, Village Outreach and we also go into partnership with children ministries that are available in our land for skillful and effective ministry.

Challenges

We have thousands of Idoma Children still without Christ and cannot read the Idoma Language Bible, our children has a right to salvation and exposure to Idoma Language.
