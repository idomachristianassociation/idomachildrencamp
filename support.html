SUPPORT US

At Idoma Christian Association, our hearts burn with a passion to win the souls of children for God. We believe in the transformative power of God’s Word and are committed to reaching out to young hearts through initiatives like the Idoma Children Camp. This camp is not just an event—it’s a sacred opportunity to plant seeds of faith in children’s lives, nurturing them with love, biblical teachings, and Christ-centered guidance. Inspired by Proverbs 22:6, we strive to "train up a child in the way he should go," so they may grow to live purposefully in God’s grace. Your support makes this mission possible. Whether through prayers, financial contributions, or spreading the word, you play a vital role in bringing children closer to God. Together, we can light the path for the next generation to walk boldly in faith. Thank you for standing with us as we follow Christ’s call in Matthew 19:14: “Let the little children come to me.” May God bless you abundantly!
